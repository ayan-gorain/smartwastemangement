The Problem Statement

WASTE PROBLEM

India generates around 62 million tons of waste annually, of which only 75-80% is collected, and less than 25% is processed. The remaining waste, approximately 31 million tons, ends up in landfills.

With rapid urbanization, the country's waste generation is projected to reach 165 million tons by 2030, making sustainable waste management a critical challenge.

Problem Solution

WASTE SOLUTION

Recyclean tackles waste issues by offering enjoyable features for waste management. Its diverse and effective set of features not only addresses the problem but also improves the overall waste management experience.
